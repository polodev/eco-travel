# Sri Lanka Tourist Visa Requirement

## Required Documents

- **Valid passport** (minimum 6 months validity with at least 2 blank pages)
- **Present address**
- **Round Trip flight ticket booking**
- **Hotel booking** (can be non-paid)
- **Occupation**
- **Contact number**
