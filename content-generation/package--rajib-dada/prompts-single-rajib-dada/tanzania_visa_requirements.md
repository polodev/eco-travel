# Tanzania Visa Requirements

## Basic Information

- **Scanned copy of Passport** with 06 months validity
- **Scanned copy of Photo** (White background)
- **Visiting card**
- **Present Address**
- **Permanent Address**
- **Entry Port-Exit Port** with Date
